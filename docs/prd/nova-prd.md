## Nova — The World's First AI Business Operator for Commerce

- **Product:** Dakio
- **Feature:** Nova
- **Version:** V1 Vision
- **Author:** Product Team
- **Status:** Vision & North Star

> This document is the **product north star**. It describes what Nova should
> become, not what any single phase ships. The engineering blueprint
> (`docs/blueprint/`) turns it into phased, testable work; the capability
> tracker (`docs/prd/capability-matrix.md` + `docs/prd/capabilities/`) records,

---

## Vision

> Nova is not an AI assistant.
>
> Nova is a full-time digital employee that can independently operate, grow, and
> optimize an ecommerce business 24/7.

Every Dakio store gets one employee from day one. Whether the founder is online,
sleeping, or on vacation, Nova continuously works to increase revenue, reduce
operational work, and improve customer experience.

The long-term vision is simple:

> One founder. One AI employee. One profitable business.

---

## Mission

Enable anyone to start and scale an ecommerce business without hiring employees.

Instead of learning marketing, sales, customer support, inventory, logistics,
finance, analytics, and operations… Nova becomes the expert in every department.

## Core Principles

### 1. Proactive First

Never wait for prompts.

- **Bad:** "Ask Nova"
- **Good:** Nova says: "I found three opportunities."

### 2. Context Aware

Nova remembers: customers, orders, products, inventory, marketing, ads,
suppliers, margins, shipping, support, conversations, analytics — everything.

### 3. Execution over Suggestions

- Don't say: "You should…"
- Instead: "I already prepared it."
- Even better: "I already completed it."

### 4. Explain Every Decision

> "I paused Campaign A because CPA increased 43% over the last three days."

## Autonomy Levels

| Level | Name | Behavior |
|---|---|---|
| 0 | Observation Only | No actions. Only reports. |
| 1 | Recommendations | Creates tasks. Needs approval. |
| 2 | Prepared Actions | Creates ads, products, posts, replies, discounts. Owner approves. |
| 3 | Autonomous | Executes low-risk actions automatically. Notifies owner. |
| 4 | Business Operator | Runs daily operations with minimal supervision. Owner sets guardrails. |

## Nova Departments

### 1. CEO
Business overview · growth planning · revenue forecasting · goal tracking ·
morning reports · weekly strategy.

### 2. Marketing
Campaign ideas · reels · posts · stories · email · SMS · push notifications ·
seasonal promotions · trend analysis · performance optimization.

### 3. Sales
Replies to customers · negotiates · applies discounts · upsells · cross-sells ·
bundles · recovers abandoned carts · handles objections.

### 4. Customer Support
Answers FAQs · tracks orders · processes replacements · refund workflows ·
escalates difficult cases · maintains tone of voice.

### 5. Product Research
Finds trending products · evaluates competition · estimates demand · suggests
pricing · generates product pages · generates creatives · imports products.

### 6. Inventory
Predicts stock · reorders · identifies dead stock · suggests bundles · supplier
switching.

### 7. Supplier Manager
Compares suppliers · negotiates prices · monitors quality · detects delays ·
switches suppliers automatically (when allowed).

### 8. Courier Manager
Chooses best courier · predicts delivery delays · reduces RTO · optimizes
shipping costs.

### 9. Finance
Profit tracking · cashflow · ad spend · taxes · margins · forecasting · expense
monitoring · alerts.

### 10. Growth
Continuously asks: "How can we grow?" Finds new channels, products, bundles,
influencers, affiliates, pricing experiments.

## Communication Style

Instead of: "There are 14 abandoned carts."

Say:

> "I prepared recovery messages for all 14 abandoned carts and expect 3–5
> recoveries if sent today."

Instead of: "Sales dropped."

Say:

> "I found the reason sales dropped: CTR decreased, supplier delayed
> deliveries, two products are out of stock. Here's what I've already done…"

## North Star Experience

A founder opens Dakio at 8:00 AM. Nova says:

> Good morning, Founder.
>
> While you were sleeping, I replied to 84 customer messages, recovered 11
> abandoned carts, published 2 social posts, identified 7 trending products,
> paused an underperforming campaign, adjusted prices on 14 SKUs based on
> competitor changes, and prepared tomorrow's marketing plan.
>
> Today's focus: Scale the "Portable Blender" campaign. If approved, I estimate
> a 12–18% increase in daily profit over the next 7 days.

The founder's reaction shouldn't be, "That's a useful AI feature." It should be:

> "I genuinely don't know how I ran my business before Nova."
